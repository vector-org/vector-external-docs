
# Developer FAQ

<details>
<summary>How do I integrate the Vector plugin?</summary>
To integrate the Vector plugin, follow the integration guide for your engine (Unity or Unreal) in this documentation. Place the plugin files in your project, configure your Game UUID, and ensure the required binaries are included in your build output.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What are the requirements for listing?</summary>
Games must meet platform standards for genre, theme, and metadata, use the official Vector plugin, and complete KYC verification. See the listing requirements section for details.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I update my game or plugin?</summary>
Update your game or plugin through the developer portal. Follow the lifecycle guide for version control and updates.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What should I do if validation fails?</summary>
Check your Game UUID, ensure the correct binaries are present, and verify your DRM token. See troubleshooting for more help.
</details>
