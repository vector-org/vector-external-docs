# Getting Started with VECTOR

Welcome to **VECTOR** — your new way to play, own, and manage games.  
This guide will walk you through setup, purchasing, and playing your first game. Each section explains not only *what* to do, but also *why* it matters, so you feel confident from the very first click.  

> **Quick Start in 5 Steps**  
> 1) Install the **VECTOR Client**  
> 2) **Create Account** (username, recovery phrase, password)  
> 3) **Browse & Buy** a game in the **Store**  
> 4) **Install** it from your **Library**  
> 5) **Play** instantly  

---

## 1. Create an Account

Before you can dive into games, you’ll need your VECTOR account. The process is quick, and it ensures that your purchases, saves, and library are tied to you no matter which device you use.  

### What you’ll need
- A desktop computer (Windows/macOS/Linux).  
- A secure way to **back up your Recovery Phrase** (we strongly recommend writing it down on paper or using a reputable password manager).  

### Steps
1. Open the **VECTOR Client**.  
2. Choose **Create Account**.  
3. Pick a **username**. This will be your display name across the platform.  
4. Select whether to **Generate** or **Import** a Recovery Phrase:  
   - **Generate** → VECTOR creates a brand-new secure phrase for you.  
   - **Import** → Enter an existing phrase if you already have an account you want to restore.  
5. Carefully back up your **Recovery Phrase**. This is your master key to the account.  
6. Create a strong **password** and accept the **Terms & Conditions** to complete setup.

![Account creation step 2](/img/gamers/Step%202.1.png)  
![Account creation step 3](/img/gamers/Step%203.png)  
![Account creation step 4](/img/gamers/Step%204.png)  

**Why this matters**  
Unlike a simple login, your Recovery Phrase is the ultimate safeguard. If your device fails, you reinstall your system, or you forget your password, this phrase is what allows you to get your account back. Think of it as the digital equivalent of a physical game key — whoever has it controls the account.

**Tips**
- Use a password unique to VECTOR, not reused from other accounts.  
- Store your Recovery Phrase offline, away from prying eyes.  
- Do not screenshot or email the phrase — those methods are too easily compromised.

## 2. Log In / Out

Once your account exists, logging in brings your entire library and purchase history into the client.  

### Log In
- At startup, the client prompts for your password. Enter it and your library syncs automatically.  

### Log Out
1. Click on the **Logout** button on the bottom of the left menu.  
3. Confirm the choice or cancel — this protects your account when sharing a device.  

![Logout screen](/img/gamers/logout.png)

**If you can’t log in**  
Use **Import Recovery Phrase** to restore your account, even on a fresh device.  

**Security Tip**  
If you’re on a shared or public computer, always log out when finished. Games will remain installed locally, but nobody will be able to launch them without your credentials.

## 3. Browsing the Store

The **Store** is your hub for discovering and buying new games. It’s designed to make exploration enjoyable while also helping you find exactly what you’re looking for.  

### Ways to discover
1. **Featured Carousel** → A rotating highlight reel of staff picks and trending titles.  
2. **Search** → Type any keyword or game title to instantly filter results.  
3. **Filters / Sort** → Narrow games by price, release date, popularity, or supported platforms.  

### Inside a Game Page
Every title has its own detailed page:  
- **Overview** → Synopsis, highlights, price.  
- **Media** → Trailers and screenshots you can click through.  
- **System Requirements** → Tabs for Windows, macOS, and Linux.  

![Game page](/img/gamers/From%20Store.jpg)

**Tips**
- Unsure what to play? The featured section is the best place to start.  
- Sorting by **Price** is a quick way to discover budget-friendly gems.  
- Always check the **System Requirements** tab before buying to make sure your machine can run it.

## 4. Buying a Game

Buying a game on VECTOR is a smooth, in-client process.  

### Steps
1. Open a **Game Page**.  
2. Click **Buy**.  
3. The **Buy Modal** appears with price, details, and a checkbox for terms & conditions.  
4. Confirm your purchase with **Buy Now**.  

![Buy modal confirm](/img/gamers/Buy%20Modal%20-%20Step%201%20-%20Confirm.jpg)

You’ll see one of three transaction statuses:  
- **Processing** → Payment is underway.  
- **Success** → Your game is now owned.  
- **Failed** → You’ll be prompted to **Retry** or cancel.  

![Buy modal success](/img/gamers/Buy%20Modal%20-%20Step%203%20-%20Transaction%20Complete.jpg)

**After purchase**  
Owned games appear instantly in your **Library** with the option to install immediately or later.

## 5. Your Library

The **Library** is your personal collection — every title you’ve bought or redeemed is visible here.  

### Filtering & finding games
- Use the **search bar** to jump to a specific game.  
- Apply **chips** to narrow by:  
  - **Installed** games  
  - Platform filters: **Windows / macOS / Linux**  

### Game Card Actions (States)

Each game is represented by a **card** that changes based on the game’s state:  

- **Not Installed** → Shows an **Install** button. Click to begin downloading and preparing the game for play.  
- **Installed** → The button changes to **Play**, launching the game immediately.  
- **Update Available** → An **Update** button ensures you stay current with the latest patches and improvements.  

This dynamic approach means you always know exactly what’s possible with each title at a glance. Hover over a card for quick actions or click through to see more details.

**Tips**
- Filtering to **Installed** is a quick way to see what’s ready to play.  
- Switching platform filters is useful if you dual-boot or use multiple devices.

## 6. Downloads

Whenever you install or update a game, the **Downloads** page keeps everything organized.  

### Manage active downloads
- **Pause / Resume / Cancel** at any point.  
- Change priority by moving items **Up**, **Down**, or straight **to the Top** of the queue.  

### Download card
Each active task shows its progress bar, estimated time remaining, and controls.  

![Downloads tab](/img/gamers/Downloads.jpg)

**Tips**
- Pause a large update if you need bandwidth for streaming or video calls.  
- Smaller titles can be pushed to the top of the queue so you’re playing sooner.

## 7. Updates

Keeping VECTOR itself updated ensures you always have the newest features and bug fixes.  

### Check for updates
1. Open **Settings**.  
2. Click **Check for Updates**.  
3. If one is available, click **Download & Install**.  

If the update fails, you’ll be offered a **Retry**. Cancelling will keep you on the current version until you try again.  

**Tip**  
It’s best to check for updates before long gaming sessions — that way you won’t be interrupted mid-play.

## 8. Settings

The **Settings** menu is your control center.  

### Account & Security
- **View Recovery Phrase** → Requires your password for safety.  
- **Change Password** → Update your credentials at any time.  
- **Copy Account Address** → Useful if support asks for your ID.  

### Preferences
- **Change Language** → Switch between supported languages.  
- **Check for Updates** → Manually trigger a client update check.  

**Tip**  
Make a habit of verifying your Recovery Phrase backup every few months. It’s your safety net if you lose access to your device.  

---

## 9. Connectivity & Offline Mode

VECTOR is built for online play, but it won’t leave you stranded when your internet cuts out.  

### When the network drops
- A clear connectivity message will appear.  
- You can **Retry** connection or switch to **Offline Mode**.  

### While offline
- You can still browse your installed games.  
- Online actions (purchases, syncing, new downloads) will be paused until you reconnect.  

### Going back online
Switch back to **Online Mode** to resume syncing, purchases, and downloads.  

**Tip**  
If you move between Wi-Fi networks or tether to your phone, give VECTOR a few seconds after reconnecting before retrying downloads or purchases.  

---

## 10. Troubleshooting

VECTOR is designed to be stable, but issues can happen. Here are the most common fixes:  

- **Game won’t launch** → Restart the client; check for pending updates in Library.  
- **Download stuck** → Pause → Resume. If that fails, cancel and restart the download.  
- **Purchase failed** → Retry the transaction; if it persists, check your payment method.  
- **Lost access** → Restore your account with your Recovery Phrase.
