# Getting Started with VECTOR

Welcome to **VECTOR** — your new way to play, own, and manage games.  
This page walks you through setup and the essentials. Use the sidebar or the **Prev / Next** links at the bottom of each section to jump around.

---

## 1. Create an Account
<a id="create-account" />

To start using **VECTOR**, set up your account:

1. Open the **VECTOR Client**.
2. Choose **Create Account**.
3. Enter your **username**.
4. Choose **Generate** or **Import** a Recovery Phrase.
   - **Generate** → VECTOR creates a secure phrase for you to back up.
   - **Import** → Enter an existing phrase to restore an account.
5. Back up your **Recovery Phrase** safely.
6. Create a **password** and accept the **Terms & Conditions**.

## 2. Log In / Out
<a id="login" />

- On startup, enter your **password** to log in.
- To log out:
  1. Open the **main window menu**.
  2. Select **Log Out**.
  3. Confirm.

> *Main window view:* `Downloads.jpg`

---

## 3. Browsing the Store
<a id="store" />

1. Go to the **Store** tab.
2. Use search, filters, or sorting (popularity, price, release date).
3. Browse the **Featured Carousel** for highlights.
4. Click a **game card** to open its **Game Page** (trailer, screenshots, system requirements).

> `From Store.jpg`

---

## 4. Buying a Game
<a id="buy" />

1. On a game page, click **Buy**.
2. Review details in the **Buy Modal** and accept terms.
3. Complete the transaction.
4. You’ll see a **Transaction Complete** confirmation.

> `Buy Modal - Step 1 - Confirm.jpg`,  
> `Buy Modal - Step 3 - Transaction Complete.jpg`

---

## 5. Your Library
<a id="library" />

All owned games appear in **Library**.

- **Filter** by installed status or OS.
- Hover a card for quick actions:
  - **Install** (if not installed)
  - **Play** (if installed)
  - **Update** (when available)

> `03 - Library.jpg`

---

## 6. Downloads
<a id="downloads" />

Manage installs and updates in **Downloads**:

- **Pause / Resume / Cancel** downloads.
- **Reorder** the queue (move up/down or send to top).
- Monitor speed and progress on the **download card**.

> `Downloads.jpg`

---

## 7. Updates
<a id="updates" />

Keep the **VECTOR Client** up to date:

1. Open **Settings** → **Check for Updates**.
2. If an update is available, **download** and **install**.
3. If it fails, retry — the client handles safe retries.

---

## 8. Settings
<a id="settings" />

From **Settings** you can:

- **View Recovery Phrase** (password confirmation required).
- **Change Password**.
- **Copy Account Address**.
- **Change Language**.
- **Check for Updates**.

---

## 9. Connectivity & Offline Mode
<a id="connectivity" />

If your network drops:

- VECTOR will surface connection errors.
- Retry syncing, or switch to **Offline Mode**.
- Switch back to **Online Mode** when ready.

---

## 10. Troubleshooting
<a id="troubleshooting" />

- **Game won’t launch?** Restart the client and check for updates.
- **Lost access?** Restore with your **Recovery Phrase**.
- Need help? Visit **hello@playonvector.com**.
