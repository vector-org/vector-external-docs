# Gamer FAQ

<details>
<summary>How do I install the VECTOR client?</summary>
Download the VECTOR client for your platform from the official website and follow the installation guide in this documentation.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What platforms are supported?</summary>
VECTOR supports Windows (x64), macOS (Coming Soon), and Linux (Coming Soon).
</details>

<details>
<summary>What should I do if a game does not launch?</summary>
Ensure the VECTOR client is running, and your game files are up to date. See troubleshooting for more help.
</details>

## Account & Security

<details>
<summary>Can I change my Recovery Phrase later?</summary>
No — you can generate a new account, but an existing Recovery Phrase is not editable.
</details>

<details>
<summary>I lost my Recovery Phrase but I’m still logged in. What should I do?</summary>
Go to **Settings → View Recovery Phrase** to back it up. You’ll need to enter your password to reveal it.
</details>

---

## Store & Purchases

<details>
<summary>Do I need to be logged in to browse the Store?</summary>
Yes, you need to be logged in and have an active internet connection to browse the VECTOR store.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What happens if a purchase fails?</summary>
The checkout modal will allow you to retry the transaction. If you cancel, you’ll remain on the game page and won’t be charged. Make sure you have enough VCTR in your wallet to complete the transaction.
</details>

---

## Library

<details>
<summary>Can I move a game to another drive?</summary>
Uninstall the game, then reinstall and select your desired drive or folder during installation (if supported by your OS).
</details>

---

## Downloads

<details>
<summary>A download seems stuck. What should I do?</summary>
Pause the download and resume. If the issue persists, cancel and restart it from your Library card.
</details>
